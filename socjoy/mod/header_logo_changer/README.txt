Header Logo Changer
########################################################################################


1) This plugin simply provides you to change your header logo easily. 

2) Prepare a logo.png as you desired and paste it into mod/header_logo_changer/grapichs/ directory. 

3) If you wish the change the style or size of the logo image, 
you will find the relevant line into mod/header_logo_changer/views/default/page/element/header_logo.php

4) I gave some additional information into header_logo.php file.
